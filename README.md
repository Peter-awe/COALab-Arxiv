> *A shared space for lab members to discover, recommend, and discuss research papers.*

## ✨ What is this?

COALab Arxiv is a lightweight, community-driven paper recommendation board built for our lab. It provides a simple way for members to share papers they find interesting, along with brief notes on why they matter.

All submissions are preserved as a growing knowledge base — a living record of the ideas and conversations that shape our research.

## 🚀 How It Works

```
📝 Submit          →    📊 Store          →    🌐 Display
Google Form              Google Sheet             GitHub Pages
```

1. **Submit** — Fill out the [Google Form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSettwuOWiGAP9YQgLcgMGdE1tLSzb-CRQ83KZFOoZpdKCewkA/viewform) with the paper details
2. **Store** — Responses are automatically saved to a Google Sheet
3. **Display** — The [website](https://peter-awe.github.io/COALab-Arxiv/) fetches the data and renders it in real time

No accounts needed. No setup required. Just submit and share.

## 📋 Submission Fields

| Field | Required | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| **Submitter** | ✅ | Select your name from the dropdown |
| **Paper Title** | ✅ | Title of the paper |
| **Paper Link** | ✅ | Link to the paper (arXiv, DOI, etc.) |
| **Abstract** | ❌ | Brief abstract or summary |
| **Tell me something interesting** | ❌ | Why this paper caught your eye |
